Judicial Translation

Documentation from any order or process relating to civil proceedings – for example, a certificate of birth or marriage, identity document, arrest, land deed, or lawsuit – needs to be judicially translated and requires a sworn translation. We have several in-house translators who do an excellent job providing court-accredited translations of all documentation mentioned above.
