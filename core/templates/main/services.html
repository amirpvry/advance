Plant Varieties

Plant variety rights, alternatively referred to as a "plant breeder's privilege," is a form of intellectual property right provided to the breeder of a new plant variety. To determine the breeder's right, prior examination by the concerned authority is required. Despite the novelty of plant varieties, we provide a variety of services to protect and enforce these rights.

Copyright

Copyright is a term that refers to the rights that creators have over their original artwork, especially artistic and literary works, regardless of their mode of composition, function, or nature. Due to its wide scope of use, copyright is often used as a broadened compliance tool in the defense of copyright law, especially in light of the Berne Convention's Automatic Protection Principle.
